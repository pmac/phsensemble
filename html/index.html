Requesting a Performance from the United States Public Health Service Music Ensemble

We are providing this page for easy access to our request form until our website is restored.

Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) and non-HHS organizations are eligible for requesting performances by the USPHS Commissioned Corps Music Ensemble.

Instructions:

  1. Review the information on the Performance Request Submission Process, found on page 1 and 2 of the Performance Request Form.
  2. Complete pages 3 and 4 of the Performance Request Form.
  3. E-mail the completed Performance Request Form to request@phsensemble.org. To do so, manually save the form then email it, or simply print, scan, and email the completed form.

    4. Click here to access the Performance Request Form.
